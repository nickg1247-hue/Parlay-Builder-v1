Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Overview

NTG Sports (“we”, “us”) provides sports analytics, model picks, and research tools. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it when you use our website.

Information we collect

  • Account data — email address and hashed password when you register.
  • Preferences — teams and players you follow, alert settings, prop slip data stored in your browser.
  • Usage data — standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and reliability.

How we use information

  • Authenticate your account and gate access to subscriber features (e.g. player props).
  • Send verification and optional digest emails you opt into.
  • Improve models, fix bugs, and maintain the service.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies & local storage

We use a signed session cookie when you sign in. Prop slip legs and team follows may be stored locally in your browser before syncing to your account.

Third-party services

We fetch public sports data, odds, and news headlines from external APIs and RSS feeds. Those providers have their own privacy policies. Email delivery may use SMTP or a transactional provider.

Your choices

  • Request account deletion by contacting us at the email on our site.
  • Clear browser local storage to remove unsynced preferences.
  • Disable digest emails in My Team settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy: use the contact method listed on the NTG Sports homepage footer.
